MALLAREDDY ENGINEERING COLLEGE FOR WOMEN


ABOUT COLLEGE



Malla Reddy College of Engineering for Women (MRCEW), also known as MRECW, is a women's engineering college located in Maisammaguda, Hyderabad. Established in 2008, it is an autonomous institution affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad. MRCEW offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA) programs.Autonomous Status: MRCEW has been an autonomous institution since 2018, granting it more control over its curriculum and academic calendar. Accreditations: The college is NAAC accredited with an A grade and has received NBA accreditation for its programs. Affiliation: It is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). AICTE Approval: MRCEW is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Diverse Programs: MRCEW offers a range of engineering programs including ECE, CSE, IT, and specialized branches like AI&ML, IoT, Cyber Security, and Data Science. Focus on Industry Readiness: The college emphasizes application-oriented teaching, problem-based learning, and provides pre-placement training programs, e-learning, and industry-connect initiatives.
